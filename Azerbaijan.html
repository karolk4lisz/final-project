National Dish of Azerbaijan



Sadj

With its countless delicious and fantastic dishes, Azerbaijani cuisine is rich in various kinds of meat, vegetable delicacies, complemented by various savory spices. The country retains some of the old cooking methods. Copper or clay pots are traditionally used and give a wonderful flavor to a meal. One dish which is always served in a special pan is called Sadj.


Where to eat the best Sadj in Azerbaijan

Shakh Garden Restaurant, Asef Zeynalli Street, Baku

Shakh Restaurant is located in the middle of old town in Baku. The restaurant offers a wide range of traditional dishes, delicious and aromatic including Sadj


Traditional Recipe for Sadj


Ingredients

250 grams of lamb or chicken

300 grams of onion

saffron on the tip of a knife

Half a teaspoon of thyme

100 grams of clarified butter or ghee

2 sweet peppers

2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes

1 aubergine

1 tomato

Herbs

Salt and black pepper

Method

1. Cut the meat into small pieces

2. Heat the butter in the pan and fry the meat for five to seven minutes until it is semi-cooked

3.Add the onion, pepper, saffron, thyme, salt and pepper, and fry everything for 10 to 15 minutes until done.

4.Fry the aubergine and tomato separately

5.Place the ingredients on the saj with the cooked meat and onion in the middle and the vegetables at the side. In a restaurant the saj is brought to the table standing on a brazier.
